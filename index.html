What is the RPS?

the RPS program—Renewable Portfolio Standards.

It's a government-funded program offering benefits like a 30% ITC (Investment Tax Credit) and a net metering system. This allows you to go solar with no money out of pocket while reducing your electric bill by 30-50%.
